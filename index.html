About Dawn Machina

Dawn Machina is a Brantford-based cybersecurity company focused on supporting Ontario's advanced manufacturing and smart infrastructure sectors. Our flagship product, DawnTrust™, is a structured planning engagement that helps organizations map cyber risk, align to standards like IEC 62443, and prepare for secure technology adoption — without requiring invasive tools or heavy integration.

What is DawnTrust™?

A 5-module planning product designed to prepare OT environments for secure modernization — including vendor integration, risk clarity, and funding readiness. Delivered as a fixed-scope engagement, not a tool or consulting service.

  • ✅ Vendor-ready integration maps
  • ✅ Trust zone and risk discovery
  • ✅ Standards alignment (Purdue 2.0, IEC 62443)
  • ✅ Visual executive deliverables
  • ✅ Grant-friendly procurement overlays

Five-Part Delivery Framework

  1. Module 1: OT Risk Discovery & Trust Mapping
  2. Module 2: Standards Overlay & Gap Review
  3. Module 3: Vendor-Aligned Integration Strategy
  4. Module 4: Phased Roadmap & Investment Timing
  5. Module 5: Executive Enablement Toolkit

Why It Matters

  • For Manufacturers: Achieve visibility, secure funding, and plan securely without disruption
  • For Vendors: Faster onboarding and alignment to site readiness
  • For Funders: Roadmaps aligned to real risk and real standards — not guesswork
